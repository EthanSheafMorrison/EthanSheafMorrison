PROJECT

He Rito

DATE

2023

ROLE

Branding, Design

TYPE

Branding, Design, Typography

OVERVIEW

He Rito is a brand identity for a Māori photography exhibition. The exhibition is a collection of photographs taken by Māori photographers.

OUTPUT

The results culminated in a Typographic and Visual Identity for the exhibition. Hosted at Toi Poneke in Wellington, New Zealand.
