Finding Kuku

2024 Research & Design

Overview

Finding Kuku is a research and design project exploring the intersection of cultural heritage and digital technology. The project investigates how digital tools can be used to preserve and share indigenous knowledge systems.

Process

Research

Initial investigation into cultural preservation methods and digital archiving techniques.

Design

Development of interactive interfaces for knowledge sharing and preservation.

Implementation

Creation of digital tools and platforms for cultural knowledge transmission.
