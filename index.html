I am a designer, research assistant, and writer. I graduated from Te Herenga Waka Victoria University of Wellington in 2023 with a Bachelor of Design Innovation with a double major in communication and media design. I have held positions as a tutor and research assistant at Victoria University since 2021 including a position as a summer research scholar (2022-2023). Throughout my undergraduate I received multiple accolades such as the Te Tini a Rehua award (2021) and Te Pūtahi o Poutama Awhina grant (2022).
